About The Chronicles

Amplifying voices from Nepal's sacred valley

Our Mission

We are a community-driven storytelling collective dedicated to amplifying voices from Kathmandu Valley. Our mission is to preserve oral histories, document living culture, and create a platform where every community's story matters.

Founded in 2019 after the 2015 earthquakes, we recognized that while buildings could be rebuilt, countless stories were being lost. We work with local journalists, community researchers, and oral historians to ensure these narratives survive.

"We don't just tell stories. We listen, we document, we preserve, and we amplify the voices that mainstream media often overlooks."

How We Work

1

Community Partnership

We partner with local community leaders who identify important stories and narrators.

2

Oral History Collection

In-depth interviews conducted in native languages (Nepali, Newari, Tamang) with translation.

3

Story Verification

All stories are cross-verified with multiple community members for accuracy and context.

4

Community Review

Final stories are reviewed by community members before publication.
